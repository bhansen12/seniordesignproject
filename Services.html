Our Story

From the desk of Mark and Angie Gray

MGBD Parts represents our personal mission to keep Rover P6 cars on the road at cost effective prices.

We have had over 20 years of experience owning, repairing, restoring and running Rover P6 cars as well as involvement in classic car clubs at the committee level. We feature cars in all variants 2000, 2200 and 3500, saloon and estate, both in everyday and show condition. Some prize jewels of our current fleet include a 1966 Rover 2000, a 1972 Rover 2000TC export, a 1975 Rover 2200 Auto, a 1972 Rover 3500 Estoura. We also, of course, have the last P6 off the production line: the VVC 700S “the avocado” Rover.

We pour our specialist knowledge and experience into our business and are dedicated to supplying quality parts for all variants of the Rover P6 built between 1963-1977. We primarily stock brand new manufactured items, but occasionally mix in NOS (New Old Stock). We offer refurbished service exchange and second hand parts exchange. We are entirely committed to providing you with only the best service and so offer entirely free advice and technical help!

Please note that all Refurbished, NOS, and Secondhand parts are detailed as such in the store, and therefore anything else listed is a brand new manufactured item. All parts are listed by their original rover numbers that were used in the corresponding catalogues.

Our store has been live since July 2012 and new items are added daily. We offer competitive shipping rates in addition to combined shipping rates for multiple purchases. Rates are negotiable but rarely flexible. You are always welcome to call in or come collect your parts from us in person in Wolverhampton.

Please feel free to call, text or email if you don’t see what you need or if you just need technical advice. Email example@gmail.com or call +44 (0)1111 222222.

Thanks for allowing us to serve you!

Mark and Angie Gray
